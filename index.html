Our Work Process

Our work process is designed to ensure efficiency, quality, and consistency in every project we undertake.

  • First Meeting

    Welcome Each Other’s.Consultation and questionnaire.
    Clearly, identify clients’ needs and purposes, Organizational
    structures and cross-functional relationships

  • Initial Phase

    Space Measurement & analysis. Effective solutions are
    discussed at this time. Identify to incorporate into design

  • Design Proposal

    Identify to incorporate into 2D design Materials suggestions
    to work within the environment,enhancing the final layout
    Specialized areas 3D Renderings and designs to give the
    Clients an idea of the look and feel of the new Look of Spaces.

  • Planning Phase

    Quotation for the implementation of the project.
    Derails Working Drawing – Architectural, Structural, Electrical
    and Plumbing Fire Design where needed.

  • Construction

    Materials Procure and Supply Workforce and Supervisor Team
    Setup Each phase of the construction is monitored ensuring
    that the client is kept informed and up to date at all times.

  • Handover & After Sales Service

    Complete and Handover the Decorated Project to client
    with full satisfaction. Free Service within a Specified
    time and long term Service on cost to cast basis.
